We accept popular file formats such as PNG, JPEG, AI, EPS, and PDF. Ensure your design is in high resolution for the best printing results.

Production typically takes 14 business days after design approval. Delivery times vary based on location, but we strive to deliver your order as quickly as possible.

Yes, our minimum order quantity depends on the type of jersey. Contact us for details on bulk orders or specific requirements.

Since our products are custom-made, we only accept returns or refunds for defective items. Please check your order carefully before approval.
